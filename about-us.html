Our Approach


At the heart of our service is aiding businesses in fund acquisition. Through our network of pre-qualified investors with a distinct focus on future technologies and a shared commitment to fostering enhanced growth, we forge valuable connections.

Our efforts culminate in meticulously organized private luncheons or dinners, where businesses can cultivate relationships and deliver compelling pitches to both Ultra-High Net Worth Individuals (UHNWIs) and pre-qualified private investors. This setting offers an ideal platform for meaningful engagement, relationship building, and effective presentation of business propositions.

Connecting with UHNWIs

We believe that each company is unique, and there is no one-size-fits-all approach to fundraising. That's why we take a personalised approach to understanding our companies' core values, aspirations, and financial requirements. Our meticulous approach ensures we can match your business with the right investors who share your vision and goals.

At Bleu Fonces, our extensive network of UHNWIs and private investors is one of our most valuable assets. These individuals possess a wealth of experience, industry insights, and a deep appreciation for innovative ventures. Through our consortium, your company gains access to a select group of investors who are financially capable and genuinely passionate about contributing to the growth of promising enterprises.
